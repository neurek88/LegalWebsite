Stephany Pleasant Maness, J.D.

Legal Assistant | Founder | stephany@pdlawomaha.com

Stephany was born in Memphis, Tennessee, but began bleeding Husker Red in 2008 when she was admitted to the University of Nebraska - Lincoln. At the University, she dual matriculated and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and German. Stephany then enrolled in University of Nebraska College of Law in 2014, graduating with her Juris Doctorate in May of 2017. She graduated with concentrations in Human Rights and Civil Litigation. Stephany worked as a law clerk for Disability Rights Nebraska from May 2015 to present.
