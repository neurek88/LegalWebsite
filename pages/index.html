The Law Office of Dai-Klabunde, LLC

Equality Before the Law

The Law Office of Dai-Klabunde, LLC is an innovative private law firm designed to help Nebraska keep its promise to its citizens that all persons, regardless of income, can take advantage of its legal system. The Law Office of Dai-Klabunde provides individuals of low to moderate income with another option to be represented in civil litigation matters without sacrificing bill payments, college savings accounts, or retirement savings.

Other information

The Law Office of Dai-Klabunde, LLC adheres to five fundamental values in providing legal work to its clients:

  • Customer service: The law office utilizes a consultation process for each of its potential clients to ensure that the needs of the client are clearly identified at the outset, so that clients feel their goals are being met.
  • Professionalism: The Law Office has policies and procedures in place to ensure quality work is done efficiently.
  • Diversity: The Law Office actively seeks to encourage diversity through involvement and engagement in communities throughout Omaha and greater Nebraska.
  • Community: The Law Office is dedicated to serving the Omaha community by adhering to Nebraska Bar Associations suggested per year pro-bono hours and recognizing associates who go above and beyond recommendations.
  • Justice: The Law Office will above all else ensure its policies provide access to justice to individuals of low to moderate income.
